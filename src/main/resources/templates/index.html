Captain Ironpot's Seafood Feast

  • For the Marinade:
    • 1/4 cup olive oil
    • 3 cloves garlic, minced
    • 2 tablespoons fresh lemon juice
    • 1 teaspoon fresh thyme, chopped
    • 1 teaspoon fresh rosemary, chopped
    • Salt and pepper to taste
  • For the Seafood:
    • 1 pound jumbo shrimp, peeled and deveined
    • 1 pound fresh scallops
    • 1 pound mussels, cleaned and debearded
    • 1 pound clams, cleaned
    • 1 pound fresh salmon fillet, cut into portions
    • 1 pound fresh cod fillet, cut into portions
  • For the Sauce:
    • 2 tablespoons butter
    • 1/4 cup white wine
    • 1/4 cup chicken or seafood broth
    • 1/4 cup heavy cream
    • Zest of one lemon
    • Salt and pepper to taste
    • Fresh parsley, for garnish

Captain Jack Ironpot's treasure secret recipe, passed down through generations, is a well-guarded culinary gem concealed within the depths of a long-lost recipe chest. Legend has it that this recipe is the key to unlocking the exquisite flavors that Captain Ironpot once crafted, combining the exotic and the ordinary to create unparalleled culinary masterpieces.
